Music

Deftones have been playing music since the early 1990s. The band uses a combination of different instruments and styles including rock instruments and turntable. The band is considered a an alternate metal band, and is known for their unique sound and style. The grammy-winning band has been releasing albums for more than 20 years and the latest album is called Ohms, released in 2020.
